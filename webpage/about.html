About Us

Our bookstore was started in 1923 by Mr. George Welsh. It was purely his love for books that made him start the store, which was initially just a book stand with a dozen books. It's growth was slow but steady. We have now grown to be a successful brand with a great showcase of books. Peruse our collection either at our store or online at the comfort of your home. Happy reading!
